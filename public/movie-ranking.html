ColinJ's Favorite Movies circa June 2023

My top 3 war movies:

Blackhawk Down

Excellent direction of a huge high-power cast. Perfect use of soundtrack to create mood and almost act as a supplemental narrator. Excellent and impactful action.

Thin Red Line

Fantastic direction of a large high-powered cast. Hauntingly beautiful imagery and composition. Magnificent cerebral trip through war and its horrors.

Dunkirk

Stunning composition that takes full advantage of the super wide film stock it was shot on. (Also a rare achievement to shoot on film instead of employing digital filmmaking.) Masterful control of nonlinear storytelling.

My top 3 movies with devastating impact:

Requiem for a Dream

Brilliant acting, direction, composition, and storytelling. Adapted from a bleak and radical 1960s novel, the film retains the novel's '60s slang-drenched dialog. Ellen Burstyn's most masterful dramatic performance.

Blindness

Devastating pandemic contagion dystopia. Its horrors were more acute when it was released because it predates the Covid Pandemic by at least a decade. Masterfully directed, shot, and acted.

Rabbit-Proof Fence

Gorgeous film adaptation of one family's biographical journey through Australia's brutal anti-miscegenation law and practices towards Aborigines. A perfect marriage of gorgeous cinematography and haunting score with indigenous tribal overtones. A must see!
